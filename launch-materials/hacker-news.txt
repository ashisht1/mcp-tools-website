Hi HN!

I built MCPTools.dev to solve a personal pain point: editing, formatting, and gathering configurations for Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers.

It includes:
1. An interactive config generator that color-codes your unified mcp_config.json block in real-time.
2. Direct "Copy JSON" configuration blocks for top servers (GitHub, Postgres, Slack, Kite) so you don't have to write templates from scratch.
3. A curated index of free/paid learning paths (classified by topic and experience level).

It is built on pure static HTML/CSS/JS (no heavy framework overhead) and is fully open source. 

Would love your feedback on what features or servers you'd like added next!
